About the Project

Problem Statement

Motion blur is a common issue in images captured under real-world conditions, caused by factors like camera shake, object movement, or low-light conditions requiring longer exposure. Traditional deblurring techniques often fail to generalize well across diverse scenarios.

Our Solution

We leverage Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) to develop a robust deblurring model that can handle various types of motion blur. Our approach combines the power of deep learning with adversarial training to achieve state-of-the-art results in image restoration.

Key Features

  • Advanced GAN architecture for high-quality deblurring
  • Handles various types of motion blur
  • Fast and efficient processing
  • User-friendly web interface
